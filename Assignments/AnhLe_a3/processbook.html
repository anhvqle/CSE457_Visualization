Questions

  1. What do you want to achieve with the visualization?

    - The visualization helps determine which gender keywords are being used the most in the story. I cleaned up all the words in all the stories
    (lowercase, remove punctuation, etc.) and count how many times each gender pronounes are being used. For example, some male keywords can be
    "men, king, husband, etc.", female keywords can be "she, wife, princess, etc.", and neutral gender keywords are "they, children, parent, etc."

    - I aim to see which keywords are being used the most in each fairy tale. This could help in conveying the general theme for each story. For
    example, if a story has "queen" and "she" being one of the most common words in the story, user will be able to tell that the story is
    about a dominant female character. Meanwhile, if the majority words are gender neutral keywords, we can assume that the story is not written
    around one single character.

  2. What tasks do you want to support?

    - It might be a good filter for user before they decide the next story that they might want to read. The visualization can help them see the
    general idea, general character of the story before they start a new book. Moreover, users will have the ability to see the gender keywords
    distributions over the story they select. A word cloud is implemented to let user browse through realy quick to see if there are any inappropriate
    words (profanity, etc.) that happen frequently thorughout the story.

  3. What designs will help you accomplish these?

    - To allow to select which story to look at the analysis, I will have a left column with all the stories listed to provide easy access to available
    books. Moreover, when choosing a story, I will have indicator (color changing, "üëâüèº" icon, etc.) to let user know which story they are looking at.
    That is the whole purpose of the left column, to provide a list of available fairy tales.

    - On the right, I will have two - three charts, graphs indicating the most common words used in selected story. I believe a good visualization can
    be a word cloud as well as a barplot to indicate the frequency of a key word showing up in the story. Finally, I think I will implement a percentage
    chart showing the percentage of each gender keywords compared to each other. Of course the chart will be color coded for easier visualization.
